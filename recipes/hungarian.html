Hungarian Sandwich

Back to Home

Description

This Hungarian Sandwich is a delicious combination of grilled sausage, fresh vegetables, and a special sauce served on a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 Hungarian sausage
  • 1 sandwich bun
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions

Steps

  1. Grill the Hungarian sausage until cooked through.
  2. Toast the sandwich bun lightly.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the bun.
  4. Place lettuce, tomatoes, and onions on the bottom bun.
  5. Add the grilled sausage on top.
  6. Cover with the top bun and serve warm.
